ABOUT

Currently, I’m Front-End Developer at LGUSuite Inc. , I create user-centric, visually appealing interfaces for web and mobile applications. I convert design mockups into responsive UI layouts using HTML, CSS, and Angular, ensuring optimal performance across devices and browsers. My role involves writing clean, maintainable code, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and staying updated with the latest UI/UX trends.

When I’m not at work, you might find me working out, reading a book, reviewing my work, or out riding with my motorcycle.
